Our Programs

Vets 'n' Pets Program

A dedicated retreat for veterans, offering a safe space to experience the healing powers of nature, horses, and dogs. This program focuses on building trust, overcoming trauma, and fostering connections in a calm, non-judgmental environment.

Youth Club

Empowering the next generation through positive interaction with animals and nature. Our Youth Club helps young individuals develop confidence, empathy, and leadership skills through structured activities and mentorship.
